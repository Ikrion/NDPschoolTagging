# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 1/6/2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **NDP School Tagging** ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the application and services.

By using the application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Access

Our application may access the following information through Google services:

### Google Account Information

With your authorization, we may access basic Google account information, including:

- Name
- Email address
- Profile picture
- Google Account identifier

This information is used solely to identify you and provide application functionality.

### Google Sheets Data

If you grant permission, our application may access Google Sheets data that you explicitly authorize.

We use this access only to:

- Read spreadsheet data required for application functionality
- Display or process spreadsheet information as requested by you
- Perform actions necessary to provide the services of the application

We do not access spreadsheets that you have/are not authorized.

### How We Use Information

We use the information we access to:

- Authenticate users
- Provide application features and services
- Read and process spreadsheet data requested by users
- Improve application performance and reliability
- Respond to support requests

### Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We may share information only:

- When required by law
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights or security
- With service providers acting on our behalf under confidentiality obligations

### Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide the services of the application and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Any Google Account information and Google Sheets data accessed by the application will be retained no later than **September 30, 2026**. After this date, all retained user data will be permanently deleted from our systems unless a longer retention period is requested.

Users may also request deletion of their data **before September 30, 2026** by contacting us using the contact information provided in this Privacy Policy.

### Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.
